# ClipFactory Sample Audit

This is a fictional owned sample for a B2B founder interview. It demonstrates the shape of a paid Clip Audit. It is not a claim about a real client, real performance, or automatic video processing.

## Source

- Content type: founder interview
- Target platforms: LinkedIn and YouTube Shorts
- Goal: authority and lead generation
- Assumption: the client owns the recording and has permission to republish edited clips

## Caption Starters

1. Long-form content is not the bottleneck. The bottleneck is choosing the moments that can stand alone.
2. Before cutting a podcast clip, test the moment for hook, standalone value, and next-step clarity.
3. If your founder interview starts with setup, the short clip should probably start later.

## Editor Notes

- Remove host setup unless it contains the actual hook.
- Burn in subtitles with safe margins for mobile UI.
- Keep the first sentence visible and readable.
- Avoid jump cuts that remove necessary context.
- Keep any claims tied to what the speaker actually says.

## Delivery Boundary

A paid audit provides clip selection, hooks, titles, captions, platform fit, and editor handoff notes. It does not guarantee views, leads, follower growth, revenue, or platform approval.
