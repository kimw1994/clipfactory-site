# ClipFactory Buyer-Intent Clip Scorecard

Use this before editing one long-form B2B episode into short-form clips. The goal is to avoid polishing moments that are quotable but weak for the buyer or target platform.

## Five-Point Clip Filter

Score each candidate moment from 0 to 2 on each criterion.

| Criterion | 0 | 1 | 2 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| First sentence tension | Needs host setup | Some tension after context | Opens with a clear claim, objection, mistake, or promise |
| Standalone clarity | Cannot stand alone | Understandable with light caption context | Makes sense without the full episode |
| Buyer relevance | Broadly interesting | Useful to the audience | Helps the right buyer make a decision |
| Tactical value | General opinion | One useful idea | Specific workflow, example, comparison, or next step |
| Platform fit | Unclear platform | Works on one surface with edits | Clear LinkedIn, Shorts, TikTok, or Reels angle |

## Decision Rule

- 8-10 points: strong clip candidate.
- 6-7 points: maybe useful, but needs a stronger hook or caption angle.
- 0-5 points: keep it for the full episode, newsletter, blog, or carousel instead of editing it first.

## What To Mark In The Transcript

For every candidate clip, record:

- Timestamp hint.
- Exact first sentence.
- One-sentence standalone idea.
- Buyer question it answers.
- Target platform.
- Hook.
- Title.
- Caption starter.
- Editor note.

## Platform Notes

LinkedIn:

- Prioritize decision moments, contrarian lessons, founder mistakes, and buyer objections.
- Captions should make the business relevance clear before the viewer plays the video.

YouTube Shorts:

- Prioritize searchable problems, quick tactical steps, and moments that point back to the full episode.
- Titles should be direct and specific.

TikTok:

- Prioritize high-tension claims, fast setup, and visible pattern breaks.
- Avoid making the clip depend on a long business backstory.

Instagram Reels:

- Prioritize concise lessons, social proof, and saveable advice.
- Captions should be easy to skim.

## Compliance Boundary

Only process content you own or have permission to edit and republish. Do not claim guaranteed views, leads, sales, followers, revenue, virality, or platform approval.
