Owned sample audit

What a paid Clip Audit looks like before editing starts.

This is a fictional B2B founder interview example. It shows the decision layer: which moments should become clips, why they work, and how an editor should handle them.

Clip opportunity 00:07:20 - 00:08:05

Stop publishing the intro. Publish the first real objection.

Hook
"Most founder videos do not fail because the ideas are weak. They fail because the useful part starts too late."
Platform fit
LinkedIn: strong for consultants, SaaS founders, and creator-led service businesses.
Editor note
Cut straight into the objection. Use large subtitles for the first sentence and remove the host setup.
Clip opportunity 00:18:45 - 00:19:30

The 3-part short-form test for a long interview.

Hook
"If this moment cannot stand alone, it is not the first clip. It may still belong in the carousel, newsletter, or full episode."
Platform fit
YouTube Shorts: useful as a searchable teaser that sends viewers to the complete episode.
Caption
Before editing, test the moment for hook, standalone value, and next-step clarity.
